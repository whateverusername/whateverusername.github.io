Gender Reveal Baby Shower

There's a baby on board and Mom wants pickles and ice cream! A cute idea for your dessert table is to play off the old superstition that certain cravings mean boy or girl. For your "it's a boy" desserts have crunchy lollipops and sour lemon cupcakes. For "girl" desserts do chewy cookie pops and sweet cupcakes. Make a banner with the parents' choices for boy and girl names, or play up your cravings theme with a sign like "What's Ashley Craving?" Super fun! Shop This Idea!
